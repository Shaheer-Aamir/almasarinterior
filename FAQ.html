What interior services do you provide?

We specialize in gypsum ceilings, 60×60 ceiling systems, partitions, wall panels, painting works, and bathroom interior solutions for residential and commercial projects.

Do you work on both residential and commercial projects?

Yes, we handle apartments, villas, offices, shops, and commercial spaces across Dubai with the same level of quality and attention to detail.

How long does ceiling or partition work usually take?

Project duration depends on size and design, but most ceiling and partition works are completed within a few days to a week.

Do you provide material and installation?

Yes, we provide complete solutions including material supply, installation, finishing, and final clean handover.

Are your works suitable for wet areas like bathrooms?

Absolutely. We use cement partitions, moisture-resistant boards, and proper finishes designed specifically for bathrooms and wet areas.

How can I get a quote for my project?

You can contact us by phone, email, or WhatsApp. Share your requirements and site details, and our team will provide a clear and competitive quotation.
